Examples are important!!!

senior or junior

While I acknowledge Claire’s concerns for the role of inexperienced emplyees when starting a business, I contend with the choice for employing more young staff.
Firstly, not only is working experience vital, but also innovation and reformation attaches great importance to a brand-new company. Nowadays, the rapid development of technology enables business to be transformative and facilitates greater opportunities for significant breakthroughs. Consequently, the junior that can bring up fresh perspectives are in urgent demand. Futhermore, if all the businesses hire experienced veterans of the industry at the beginning, younger people will still lack the corresponding experience in the future. This perspective aligns with substainablility. For instance, tech giants like Google prefer young right-out-of-university employess to ensure continuous knowledge reinvention.In conclusion, I approve that when establishing business, hiring and collaborating with the young is more conducive span style=”color:red;”> to both disrupitve startups and substainable employment environment.

advertisment

In the information age, advertisement proliferate at an unprecendented rate especially through Internet.While I do acknowledge Brad’s approval for its utility in information providing, the perspective that at most cases, advertisement acts as a manipulator to promote purchasing is convincing for me. It is widely critisized that many manufacturers/corporations(company) tend to disseminate misleading(fake) claims through advertising campaigns and conditionally attempt to implement hunger-marketing, a tactic that deliberately produces less products to gain higher profits, aiming to make themselves attractive. However, this deceptive pratice violate business ethics(immoral) and may breach consumer-protection laws (illegal), ultimately deteriorating(harm) previously customer-friendly entrepreneurial enviornment.In conclusion, the drawbacks of advertising outweigh its advantages, therefore goverments should shoulder their administrating and supervising resonsibilities.

AI/techonology

While I acknowledge Megan’s concern that AI may exacerbate social unfairness, I contend with the opinon that AI is a powerful/innovative/tranformative rather than harmful tool to our society.As is known to us all, Artificial Intelligence has greatly enhanced/revolutionized productivity and is conducive to promoting a more innovative society/catalyzing innovation.For example, in scientific research, it has solved a great deal of difficult/complex problems ranging from math to physics, resulting in the increased efficiency in not only educational but also manufacturing industry. What’s more, what AI has replaced is the work that is (in high demand of manual harbor, or in high risk)hazardous, mundane, and repetitive. This technological shift can liberate the workforce, enabling the community to accomplish more fantastic and fulfilling projects that demand uniquely human capabilities.
