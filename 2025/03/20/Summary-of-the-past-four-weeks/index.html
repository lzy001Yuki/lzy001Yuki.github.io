我口袋只剩玫瑰一片，此行却山高路远

A bit funny, it is not until today that somebody reminded me of the blog I had set up during the winter vacation, and I realized I had almost forgotten about it. However, pessimistically speaking, maybe I’ve done nothing worth writing about. Just listening to lectures, finishing homework, preparing for the TOEFL exam (really??), being bothered by various things, and then staying confused—about myself, about the future.

No progress, honestly.

I am the kind of person who needs to be pushed to gain the motivation to accomplish a plan, but the past four weeks seemed a little too relaxed. Luckily, it’s not too late to realize this.

At the beginning of this semester, the departure of one of my best friends did have some influence on me. Even though my classmates and I have lived and studied together for the past one and a half years, her leaving still gave me a feeling of loneliness. However, it has turned out that I worried too much. Thank you all, my nice and excellent friends!

Anyway, this blog will help push me to write down learning notes (including both in and out of the class), ideas after reading some papers, and rambling thoughts about my daily life.

Oh, yeah, about my research preference: AI, I think. Reason? Just because I’d like to accomplish some concrete projects and witness them serving more people. Maybe there is still a long way to go, and many intelligent peers are working hard for it. I will persist.

Things need to change. Now is the best time.

This week was the first time I had real contact with scientific research (maybe not that concise, but definitely my first laboratory experience). Good luck to me.
